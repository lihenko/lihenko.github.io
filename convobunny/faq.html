How do you increase sales on my page?

If you are not already using chatters we will ensure that there is never a second on your page where a fan is not being catered too. We answer all messages at any time and ensure that our chatters are upselling to new and existing subscribers. We also ensure that all your expired fans are followed back and that they are still receiving your direct messages regardless if they are still subscribed to you or not. Our reach is maximum and response time immediate!

How soon can I see results?

Provided that you have the right content for us and you have a fan aquisition strategy we can guarantee immediate results!

Can I choose my own chatters?

During our 30 min free consultation call we do a complete walk through of your page and help you determine what chatter(s) would best suit the genre of your page. All our chatters are fully trained in Vanilla and Femdom chat techniques so there will never be a time a subscriber's niche or specific needs are not catered too.

How many chatters do I need?

This all depends on how many fans you have on your page and what your current ranking (%) is. If your ranking is around 5% with only a couple hundred fans then we recommend only 1 chatter. However, if you are planning to scale fast then a full time chatter team is recommended! If your ranking is a minimum 0.5% with thousand+ fans then we recommend you have 3 full time chatters working an 8 hour shift which brings your page to full 24/7 coverage.

What payment plan do you think is best for me?

We will try and establish this during the 30 min consultation call or discuss this with one of our sales representatives on telegram.

How many hours do the chatters work?

Each chatter work 8 hours per day 6 days a week

Is there guaranteed success in hiring chatters?

Absolutely! Upselling and engagement is at the core of our sales strategy. When a chatter is always present on your page to cater to subscribers requests then you are guaranteed to make more upsells vs if they were not stationed on your page at all.

Is it safe?

100%. We use single IP logins which allows chatters to login without the model having to share her password!

Where are your chatters from?

Our chatters are from all around the globe

How good is their English?

All our chatters are highly proficient in English and undergo rigourus English grammar proficiency tests and given practical tests before they are deployed as a chatter on any of our models pages.

When can I start?

After we have made an assessment of your page and established your needs and goals then we will get to work putting your chatter(s) team together. This may take a few hours but our goal is to get our chatters working on your page within 24 hours.

How long does it take to put chatters on my page?

We have a large database of chatters so there is also a team that can be put together within a short time frame.

What do you need from me if I want to start?

As soon as we receive your model questionnaire and agreed to our terms of service we can start working on your page immediately.

Can I provide an NDA?

Absolutely! We prefer if the model provides us an NDA to sign before our chatters get to work on her page.

Which plan is best for my page?

This all depends on your monthly earnings. We have 4 price plans to suit your needs but of course we can discuss this in our consultation call or through our text chat line on telegram. Please have a look at our pricing plans for more information

When should I make my first payment?

Your first payment is due before any chatters get to work on your page. If you have chosen a Flat rate + commission structure then commission owed will be paid at the end of the month and the flat rate at the beginning of the month.

Can I cancel if I no longer want the service?

You may cancel anytime

Can I get a refund if I am not happy with the service?

We are happy to provide you with a refund provided that our chatters are not making any improved sales over the course of 72 hours and there are no external issues preventing them from making sales.

Do I have to sign a contract?

If you have chosen a commission based plan then you are required to sign an agreement

Does ConvoBunny provide model management services?

We do not provide model management services. We only provide chatters and advise on how to increase sales within the platform. If you are seeking model management please our Recruitment form and we will be able to put you into contact with the many agencies that we work with.
