Invest in Urology Devices

PSSU is the Non-OEM market leader in Bipolar resection electrodes in the USA. With our focus on building and delivering brand neutral devices for use in bipolar resection PSSU products are a trusted device for use in operating rooms across the US. Some notable products are our Hemi-Vaporization Electrode, SuperNoMA and our innovative PreCise UltraThin electrode which uses a wire that is as thin as a single human hair to provide more targeted tissue removal in the bladder.
