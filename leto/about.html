About Us

Our strong team of over 100 dedicated professionals meticulously prepares orders daily, ensuring that the herbs are sorted, packed, and delivered in the best possible condition. Our team is constantly growing, and we are currently looking for strong candidates in sales, quality control, and skilled packing roles.

Our Facilities

90% of our fresh produce in Uzbekistan is grown in open fields within our own equipped spaces, and we utilise enclosed greenhouses to cultivate premium exotic herbs such as chives, sage, oregano, basil, rosemary, thyme, and more. We are certified with HACCP, ISO 22000:20018, and Global GAP, reflecting our commitment to the highest quality and safety standards.
