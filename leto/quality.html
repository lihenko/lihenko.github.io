Quality & Certifications

Quality and Safety, Guaranteed

Quality is our top priority. Our production processes are certified with HACCP, ISO 22000:2018, and Global GAP, ensuring the highest food safety and hygiene standards. We maintain an unbreakable cold chain throughout our distribution process, supported by our large fleet of vehicles, to guarantee that our products remain fresh from farm to fork.

We constantly strive to improve our processes to demonstrate our commitment to progress and the highest level of customer satisfaction. As part of this commitment, we are currently undergoing the BRCGS certification process and look forward to adding it to our list of certifications soon.
