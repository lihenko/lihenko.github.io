Our Story

Danny was my father, who lived with Parkinson's disease for over 18 years. Throughout this journey, my mother served as his primary caregiver, facing hundreds of crucial decisions about his care, finances, estate planning, and long-term care arrangements.

This deeply personal experience - watching my mother navigate the complexities of caregiving for my father - revealed a significant challenge in the caregiving landscape: the lack of consistent, reliable information and guidance across the many phases of the caregiving journey. From early diagnosis to late-stage care, we encountered fragmented resources, disparate sources of information, and no unified platform to help make well-informed decisions about care, financial planning, and emotional support.

Today, Danny helps caregivers by bringing together verified information, peer support, and decision-making tools all in one place, making an already challenging journey more manageable. Through the power of AI, we're ensuring that no caregiver has to piece together critical information from scattered sources or make important decisions without proper guidance and support.

DANNY's Founder

Richard Jordan

Founder and CEO

An accomplished digital marketer and business development professional, Richard is focused on building and expanding the future of Danny and the impacts it can have on the future of caregiver support. Prior to founding Danny, Richard was VP of Strategic Partnerships for the Financial Services brands at Red Ventures where he drove commercial growth of Bankrate and a variety of other brands. After experiencing the real life struggles of his father (Danny) battle Parkinson's disease and watching his mother be a dedicated caregiver for 18 years, Richard was inspired to make the caregiver process of learning, engaging and eventually making the multitude of non-medical decisions on behalf of a loved one more connected and attainable.

He graduated from The Citadel with a B.S. in Business Administration
