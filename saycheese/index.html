What Makes Dutch Cheese So Good?

Why Dutch cheese is such a treat is because of the food (grass/hay) the Dutch cows eat. The Netherlands are also called the lower-lands and wet- lands so the ground/earth and grass that grows on it makes the milk from the cows delicious. It is a combination of the weather and the environment where the cows graze. This makes the cheese taste special and wonderful.
