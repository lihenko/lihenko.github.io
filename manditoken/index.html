Overview

MnI specializes in investing in real-world assets, namely Indonesian conventional businesses. Our industries of focus include hospitality, mining, infrastructure, and commercial real estate. We issued Mandi tokens as a way to wrap all of these assets into one, so that the risks and value accruals are properly diversified.

Why Indonesia?

According to IMF reports analyzed by Bloomberg, Indonesia is expected to continue to be a dominating force of global growth by 2024. We have spent the last few years narrowing down our portfolio companies, which currently represent $40M in net worth, with much more to come.
Indonesia is inward-focused, meaning services and business focus on local population & tourist economy. Expansion to global markets has only recently begun.
Boasting the 4th highest population in the world, Indonesia has a highly skilled and cost-effective workforce, a stable political environment, and a wealth of untapped resources lying just a few feet beneath the soil.
