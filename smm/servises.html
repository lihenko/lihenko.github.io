SERVICES

Salt Mines Mediation is committed to helping you resolve conflict and come to an agreement while saving you time, money, animosity, and stress.

A Faster, Cost-Effective Path to Resolution and Peace of Mind

We understand that this process can be stressful, and we help you to settle your differences quickly with the best possible outcome for all parties involved. Mediation can be quicker and less costly than traditional court proceedings, allowing a smoother transition as you move forward with the next chapter of your life.

We work to help you to understand your options, foster open communication, facilitate constructive dialogue, and empower both parties to make shared decisions to reach agreements. Mediation puts you more in control of the timeline and the outcomes of your unique situation.

How Our Services Support You

Family Mediation

We focus on Domestic and Family Mediation cases, which could include divorce, child custody, and other family disputes. Mediation can include the entire case or be used to agree on certain issues.

Divorce

Navigate divorce with understanding and collaboration. We help to foster open communication and shared decisions. We will facilitate constructive dialogue and empower both parties to reach agreements on important issues like custody, support, and asset division.

Child Custody and Child Support

Our process promotes shared decision-making, allowing you to resolve custody disputes amicably. We will guide the discussions, help with understanding of both parties, and reach a decision that is tailored to the unique needs of your family.

Modification

When you need to discuss changes in circumstances such as custody arrangements, support, or visitation schedules, our streamlined approach helps to make these changes easier through productive discussions and mutually beneficial agreements.
Whatever your unique circumstances may be, mediation gives you more control over the potential outcome, the ability to speak more freely about your opinion and issues, gives you a faster resolution, and is less expensive than trial.

How the Mediation Process Works:

Contact us today for your free consultation. During this 30 minute Zoom session, we can discuss your situation and see if mediation is right for you.
Then, we will schedule your initial session, which can be held in-person or on Zoom. During this session, we will listen to each party individually and hear their personal concerns and ideal outcomes. Then, we will work to find common ground on the opposing issues. The initial session generally lasts 3-4 hours.
Once your initial session is complete, we may create a custom recommendation for you to finalize your mediation based on any outstanding issues.
