About

Save - Time, Money, Animosity, Stress.
Keep The Power & YOUR Future in YOUR Hands.

Terence Channon

I am a Florida Supreme Court Certified Family Mediator and I chose to help people through family mediation because I understand the need to have a process that’s easier to understand, less expensive, and helps to make decisions faster. I have been divorced twice, and in both cases, I maintained amicable relationships with my exes and the deals agreed upon were viable and sustainable for all involved.

Through my background as a Registered Investment Advisor, I have training and experience in various financial instruments and can help understand how the finances (usually the biggest focus point) flow between the 2 parties.

I deeply understand how finances work as well as how assets work.  Attorneys are rarely trained in financial matters and are often asking ‘teach me’ questions while their clients are on the clock.  While I will not provide specific investment advice, I will use my knowledge to expedite the negotiations.

Because of my experiences in my own divorces, I became a Florida Supreme Court Certified Family Mediator to help others. My goal is to offer insightful and effective suggestions for your unique situation, and I am committed to helping you to reach equitable agreements efficiently, saving you both time and money.
