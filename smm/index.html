How We Help You
Terence specializes in helping you to resolve your family and divorce matters without lengthy court proceedings and the stress of fees piling up.

Control Your Own Destinies

You are in control of the timing with mediation, not the Court. It is more efficient because communication happens directly between the Parties at the mediation table. Having a written agreement before you file your case can also save you time on lengthy litigation.

Reduce Expenses & Cost

When you attend mediation, you can avoid attorneys charging by the hour. You are not required to have an attorney in a Florida family law case, but with mediation you will have a clear understanding of your decisions.

More Flexibility

Can’t meet with a mediator in their normal 9-5 schedule? We offer more flexibility with evening and weekend availability, to work around your busy schedule, plus in-person or Zoom meetings for your convenience.

Our Family Mediation Services

The legal process can seem overwhelming, as there are many variables that contribute to the outcome of your case. Salt Mines Mediation provides mediation services for issues that relate to Family Meditation in Florida. Our family services include but are not limited to the following:
  • Divorce or Dissolution of Marriage
  • Child Custody and Support
  • Modification of Custody
  • Parenting Decisions
  • School Placement
  • Asset/Debt Division
  • Sale of House
  • Other Family Disputes

Our Business Mediation Services

We help you to minimize the time and effort needed to resolve business disputes through moderating meetings, finding resolutions that work for both parties, and assisting you in dissolving partnerships fairly. Our business services include but are not limited to the following:
  • Creating Contracts
  • Business Partnership Disputes
  • Vendor Disputes
  • Employee Issues

About Terence

Terence is a Florida Supreme Court Certified Family Mediator utilizing a strategic approach to negotiation with a focus on eliminating stress and uncertainty. With his background as a Registered Investment Advisor and understanding of financial intricacies, Terence works directly with you to offer insightful and effective suggestions for your unique situation. After experiencing two divorces himself, he saw the importance of mediation in reaching mutually beneficial resolutions faster, and his goal is to help you avoid costly and time-consuming combative environments. His experience equips him with a unique perspective that bridges the gap between legal and financial realms while ensuring your goals are heard and met with meaningful resolutions.

Terence is committed to helping his clients reach equitable agreements efficiently, saving them both time and money.
