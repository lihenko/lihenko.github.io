CFVI delivers CFValve technology to control flow rates at a preset pressure with a +/- 1.5% flow rate accuracy. The CFValve™ supports any process that profits from the constant, calibrated flow of liquids, air, or gasses.

CFValve™ - The Original

CFValve™ - The Original

The CFValve was designed in the 1990's to improve the precision and safety in agricultural applications. CFValve technology has evolved to now deliver unparalleled flow control. It helps to simplify operations, reduce waste, and increase automation.

The original CFValve is non-adjustable and is available in four operating pressures. It is consistently accurate even with changing input pressures and needs no calibration or tuning. More than 2,000,000 units are deployed globally - with the lowest failure numbers in the industry.

CFVI supports the following industries: Agriculture, Lawn Pest Control, Painting and Coating, Fertigation, Hydroponics, Chemical Control and Dosing, Water Conservation, Medical, Car Wash, and Industrial Cleaning.
