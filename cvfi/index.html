Precision and Reliability: CFVI's Fluid Control Solutions

CFV Innovations’ fluid control and dosing systems are designed to save money, reduce waste and deliver exceptional performance. With over 20 years of field experience in multiple industries, our proven technology delivers high-ratio mixing and metering with extreme accuracy.

Accurate.

Consistently accurate regardless of varying external conditions and no need for calibration or tuning.

Versatile.

Works across multiple industries and can achieve mixing ratios anywhere between 2:1 and 1000:1.

Proven.

Over 2,000,000 CFV units in the field and the lowest failure numbers in the industry.
