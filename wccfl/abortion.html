Abortion Pill Reversal

If you have taken the first medication in the abortion pill procedure and change your mind, there is still hope for saving the pregnancy.

HOW DOES IT WORK?

The first medication you took shut off the supply of progesterone to the fetus, the Abortion Pill Reversal regimen simply replaces that progresterone supply. Progesterone is a naturally occurring hormone your body produces to nourish the pregnancy.

HELP IS AVAILABLE NOW

Call the 24 hour helpline to be connected to a nearby medical professional. They will walk you through each step of the process and guide you as you work to reverse the effects of the abortion medication.
