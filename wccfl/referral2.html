Wellness Exam Referral

If you meet the following criteria, we can set up a free Women's Wellness Exam at a local provider.

If you…

  • Have NOT had a physical or wellness exam in the past year
  • Are a woman, age 18 or older
  • Have any state issued photo ID
  • Do NOT have private health insurance or Medicaid
Then you may be eligible for a FREE Women's Wellness Exam including Pap Exam and lab work.
