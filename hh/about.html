About Us

Healthy Harvest, LLC is a family-owned company that grows and ships high-quality watermelons throughout the U.S. With food safety-certified farms in Florida, Georgia, and North Carolina, we produce in-season watermelons in almost every month of the year. With a commitment to safe, sustainable farming practices, you can feel confident serving Healthy Harvest watermelons to your family.
