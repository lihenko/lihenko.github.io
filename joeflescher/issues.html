With citizens, with County workers, with the committees and Boards I participate in. When true communication happens everything has more of an opportunity to move forward toward a solution that can help solve problems. Joe Flescher’s General Position Statements as a Candidate for Indian River County Commission- District 2

INTEGRITY. DEDICATION. EXPERIENCE.

Indian River Lagoon

Joe Flescher pledges to continue to work hard to preserve the character of our county and to help find solutions to repair and restore the fragile eco-system that is our precious Indian River Lagoon!

TRANSPORTATION

Joe Flescher will continue to propose the best possible transportation improvements for Indian River County at the least tax payer expense possible, and to fight against any Brightline High-Speed Rail issues in regard to them creating safety issues for our county and for all businesses & residents.

CONSERVATIVE

Joe Flescher will continue to support a concervative county budget while gearing up to meet the needs of our new economic growth potential.

FISCAL RESPONSIBILITY

Joe Flescher will continue to be a watchdog for fiscal responsibility.

COMMUNICATE

Joe Flescher will continue to communicate with our county workforce to help us become the most efficient provider of services we can possibly be.

LISTEN

Joe Flescher will continue to communicate with our county workforce to help us become the most efficient provider of services we can possibly be.

DEDICATED

Joe Flescher will continue to be a dedicated solution-provider.

ECONOMIC

Joe Flescher will continue to seek a responsible path toward our sustained Economic Recovery for the betterment of every Indian River County resident.
