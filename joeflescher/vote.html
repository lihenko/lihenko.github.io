I am an Indian River County resident, but I do not live in Joe’s District. Can I still vote for Joe Flescher in the Primary election on Tuesday, August 23rd?

answer
As long as you are a registered Republican and live in Indian River County, you can vote for Joe Flescher whether you live in District #1, #2, #3, #4, or #5.

I have friends who are registered Democrats and Independents. Can they vote for Joe Flescher in the election on Tuesday, August 23rd?

answer
As of right now, every registered voter in Indian River County, regardless of their party affiliation, can vote for Joe Flescher when the Primary occurs on Tuesday, August 23rd.
