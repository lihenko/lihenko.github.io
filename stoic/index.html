THE STOIC PHYSIQUE PILLARS

DATA-DRIVEN

Using metrics to our advantage to help:

  • Take out the guesswork and help plot out a step-by-step formula specifically for you to be able to use for the rest of your life.

NUTRITION PRECISION

Taking R.A.D (Random Acts of Dieting) out of your life to bring consistent week-over-week progress by:
  • Creating a structure of meals and recipes that fit your lifestyle, preferences and everything you have going on.
  • Manipulating timing, portions and macro ratios to improve energy levels and performance in and out of the gym.
  • Working with your cravings and appetite spikes rather than against them so the results you see....last a lifetime.

TRAINING OPTIMIZATION

Not just exercising. But, training with a goal and vision by:
  • Designing a customized routine around weak points, injuries, and mobility sticking points to sculpt a balanced physique.
  • Varying training phases and periodizations within each phase to maximize progressive overload and drive muscle growth
  • Creating sustainability by balancing your recovery to fatigue ratio that may be caused by external variables (work, stress, sleep...etc).
