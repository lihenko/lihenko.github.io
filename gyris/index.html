Story

Gyris is a three-part saga. Part 1: The Sound of Trees Standing begins with a Great War between the Minjierri and the Dharwar. A dark hand is at work. Heroes fall and others forged in pain. The Beast race of the Bura births an unlikely hero. The fungus-cloaked Mara, do the bidding of The Seed Gummuri. The Ochichurri threatens to break the shackles of the dark. All that is known is at stake; and in the unknown lies a truth larger than any could imagine.

Follow Argus, Saras, Djalu and the inhabitants of Gyris in a saga spanning 3 generations. No one alone, can fight the Dark.

Our Team

The Eye (Chris Harkin)
A motion designer, FX artist, photographer and musician, inspired and fascinated by elements of the natural world, our perception of reality, the nature of consciousness, and the universe. Friends with Co-creator Muff (Adam) since high school, collaborating on music videos, music, art and now NFTs. Not only is he a genuine creative prodigy but a legendary human.
The Prophet (Adam Everill)
A father of two, social entrepreneur and creative he wrote the beginnings of Gyris in Easter 2020 for his kids and the world unfolded in bedtime tales probably not suitable for children. Adam is Cofounder and Chair of Equal Playing Field an organisation primarily working on the prevention of violence against women and the protection of children in Papua New Guinea and Australia. EPF has raised $10,000,000 to deliver their development innovations to 10’s of thousands.
The Alchemist (Saba Udzilauri)
Obsessed with math & physics and logic, Saba grew up in front of a computer screen. He is a Blockchain developer, specializing in Solidity and Web3 with a Cyber Security Engineering background. Deeply passionate about philosophy and learning about how everything around us works, from the smallest detail, to its place in a biggest picture. Discarding nihilism, he is purpose-driven and full of wonder and optimism. A perfect fit for Gyris.
King of the Beasts (The Rat King)
The Rat King is the creator of the Sewer Rat Social Club and advisor on other major sell-out projects such as Jungle Freaks. He is a world-class and proven blockchain and software developer with a deep understanding of all things decentralised. Adam reached out to the Rat King as an SRSC holder, after admiring the community, philosophy and technology, but formally asked him on as a team member after seeing his reputation to deliver and support for the NFT community.
