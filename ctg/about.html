ABOUT Us

CTG is a women-owned cleaning enterprise that services commercial buildings, hospitals, industrial facilities, and construction sites. Service the Metro Atlanta region and the entire State of Georgia, Fluff provides customer-focused cleaning and janitorial solutions that meet the specific needs of its clients.
24/7 Service
Around the clock service offerings to respond to emergency situations and build a program around your business’ schedule.
Assessment Based Solutions
CTG cleaning services are not one-size fits all. Every client’s premise is inspected, and a custom program, including cleaning techniques and product selection, is designed to meet the needs of our clients.
Training Focused
All team members are extensively educated and trained on our client-focused programs before being assigned. Our team members are experts in cleaning and on our clients’ business needs.
Highly Credentialed
CTG maintains several certifications and adheres to strict safety guidelines and best practices.
