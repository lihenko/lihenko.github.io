ABOUT US

CTG is a women-owned cleaning enterprise that services commercial buildings, hospitals, industrial facilities, and construction sites. Service the Metro Atlanta region and the entire State of Georgia, Fluff provides customer-focused cleaning and janitorial solutions that meet the specific needs of its clients.
