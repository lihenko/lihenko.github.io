Fades way too quick on me! I love how light and refreshing it is though, it is incredibly simple and easy to wear. I have some freshies that last longer but they're not as refreshing to me as Acqua di Gioia, which has a legitimate "icy" effect - mint comes through in a very cool and light way, not green or herbaceous and not loudly BLUE like cool water and other gel deodorant-like scents. The short longevity might be the price you have to pay here.

I'm not gonna buy it because I tend to collect things I find more interesting, but will keep AdG in mind as a gift for perfume newbies in my life - it's very easy to appreciate, virtually anyone can wear it.
