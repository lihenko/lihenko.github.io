Get to know BOSS USA

BOSS USA is an Oracle Utilities Partner focused on professional and managed services exclusively for Customer Care and Billing (CCB), Customer to Meter (C2M), Customer Cloud Service (CCS), Meter Data Management (MDM), Customer (CSS) and Digital Self-Service (DSS), Work and Asset Management (WAM), and Utilities Analytics. Our expertise is in system implementation, integration, upgrade, and conversion across the Oracle Utilities product portfolio of on-premises and cloud services for Electric, Gas, Water, Steam, Solar, and Internet service types.
