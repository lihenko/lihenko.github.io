About Us
Founded by Dr. Jonathan Ashdown (CEO) and Prof. Francesco Restuccia (CTO), SpectrumAI Solutions was built on the belief that artificial intelligence will redefine the wireless landscape. With decades of combined expertise in RF engineering, AI/ML, and emerging communication standards, our leadership team is committed to advancing the future of wireless technology.
Dr. Jonathan Ashdown
CEO
Jonathan Ashdown was born in Niskayuna, NY, USA. He received the B.S., M.S., and Ph.D. degrees from Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute, Troy, NY, USA, in 2006, 2008, and 2012, respectively, all in electrical engineering. His doctoral dissertation was on a high-rate ultrasonic through-wall communication system using MIMO-OFDM in conjunction with interference mitigation techniques.
