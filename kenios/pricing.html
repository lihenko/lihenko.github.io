Pricing

We believe in honest and transparent pricing.
We do not operate from an expensive HQ: we use best in class practice management software to operate on a fully remote basis, utilising office locations on an ad hoc basis as and when required. Through leveraging the best emerging technology, underpinned by creative solutions, we are able to maximise productivity and generate real savings for our clients whilst delivering a service of the highest quality.

General

Kenios is not an SRA regulated law firm. With SRA regulation there is a significant cost burden. Please see this link for guidance on the SRA’s requirements. Where regulation is required, this increased cost of regulation absolutely makes sense. Where regulation is not required, our view is that the significant costs benefit of being self-regulated should be passed on to our clients.

Whilst the company itself is not regulated, we are still able to provide SRA regulated solicitors directly to our clients and this allows us to offer first class regulated professionals at a significant discount to traditional law firms without affecting the quality of the service or the trust that our clients have in the integrity and capability of our lawyers.

Managed services for both legal and company secretarial

Where headcount is an issue our monthly subscription plans for businesses offer a viable solution so that you may accurately budget for your legal requirements.

Following an initial scoping exercise and brief trial period (to ensure both parties have accurately scoped the requirements and costs) a fixed 12-month retainer is entered into providing cost certainty to our clients. No hidden fees, no fluctuations: just transparent and predictable cost.

Our Managed Services Teams are highly productive, and you will only pay for value added legal work: there is no lost time to admin or other none chargeable activity. This allows us to offer these services for a rate that is often cheaper than you can achieve in-house (on an output basis).

Given our unique structure and the technology we deploy this is a highly cost-efficient service.

General legal services and company secretarial services

We charge on a fixed fee basis unless you ask us to charge you otherwise. All prices are based off a flat hourly rate of £185 per hour for qualified solicitors and £140 per hour for non-qualified staff. This is a huge saving on traditional law firms whilst delivering a service of the highest quality. No hidden fees, no fluctuations: just transparent and predictable cost.

Legal resourcing

Our model covers both short and long term resourcing. Pricing will vary depending on the length of assignment, nature of the work and skill set required. We find that we are able to cover these assignments at a cost often in excess of 50% cheaper than a traditional law firm would charge.
